HISTORY OF ESCUELA SECUNDARIA TECNICA MEXICO:


Escuela Secundaria Técnica México is so named because it is a gift to the people and Government of Belize by the Government of México. In response to a need to make technical/vocational secondary education available in the Corozal District, the Government of México funded the construction of ESTM. On Monday, 17th January, 1983, Escuela Secundaria Técnica México opened its doors. On Tuesday, 18th January, formal classes began with an enrolment of sixty-eight (68) students and a staff of five teachers including the first principal, Mr. Rosendo “Chendo” Urbina .
